Peercoin Color Codes & Fonts:

Please use these color codes on your Peercoin related websites whenever possible.
This will help us keep the Peercoin ecosystem and its branding and visuals consistent.

Green: #3cb054
White: #ffffff

The Peercoin text logo uses the following font:
Font: FF Mark
Type: Mark-Medium
Website: http://www.ffmark.com/

The Peercoin website and wallets use the Roboto font for its content.
